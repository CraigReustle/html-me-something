My Trip to Chicago 2015

Millenium Park


The Bean

Cloud Gate, AKA The Bean, is a public sculpture by Indian-born British artist Anish Kapoor, that is the centerpiece of AT&T Plaza at Millennium Park.



Rosie and I by The Bean


Harris Theater

Harris Theater is a 1,525-seat theater for the performing arts located along the northern edge of Millennium Park


Buckingham Fountain

Buckingham Fountain is a Chicago landmark in the center of Grant Park. Dedicated in 1927, it is one of the largest fountains in the world.


Shedd Aquarium

Largest indoor aquarium in the world, with more than 8000 aquatic animals.


Field Museum of Natural History

The Field Museum of Natural History, also known as The Field Museum, is a natural history museum in Chicago, and is one of the largest such museums in the world.

Adler Planetarium

The Adler Planetarium is a public museum dedicated to the study of astronomy and astrophysics. It was founded in 1930 by Chicago business leader Max Adler.


Gemini 12 Spacecraft

During re-etry, this spacecraft's heat sheild protected the crew from temperatures up to 3,500F.


Navy Pier

Navy Pier is a 3,300-foot-long pier on the Chicago shoreline of Lake Michigan
